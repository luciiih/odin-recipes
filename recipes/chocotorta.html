Chocotorta

A quick and easy cake with only a few ingredients!

This cake is very easy to make, a traditional desert in Argentina and perfect for any occasion

No oven needed!

Ingredients:

  • Chocolinas* cookies
  • Dulce de leche
  • Cream cheese
  • Instant coffee

Steps:

  1. Mix dulce de leche and cream cheese in equal parts in a bowl
  2. Prepare instant coffee and pour it in a deep dish
  3. Grab your cookies and soak them in coffee, make sure they don't get too soft
  4. Place the soaked cookies at the bottom of a rectangular glass dish
  5. Pour the mixture we prepared in step one over the cookies, and repeat these steps until you have at least 3 layers, or use all your filling
  6. Put it in the fridge and let it cool for at least 12 hs
  7. Enjoy!
